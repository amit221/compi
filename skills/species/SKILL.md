---
name: species
description: Show your species discovery progress — which rarity tiers you've found for each species
---

Show the species index by running the species command via Bash:

```bash
node scripts/cli.js species
```

Show the complete output to the user. Do not summarize.

The species index shows each discovered species with 8 rarity tier dots (Common through Mythic). Filled dots mean you've caught or bred a creature of that species with at least one trait at that rarity level.

Hybrids (created from cross-species breeding) appear in a separate section below base species.
