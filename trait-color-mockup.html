Design Principles

  • Creature art: Monochrome (black lines). Simple, readable, not distracting.
  • Trait backgrounds: Colored ONLY behind trait info based on score rarity.
  • Rare traits: Bright background + saturated text. Visual weight matches importance.
  • Common traits: No background, white text. Recedes visually.
  • Color concentrates meaning: One zone (trait info) holds all the quality signals.
